Frequently Asked Questions

How does the escrow system work?
When a trade is initiated, funds are securely locked in an escrow wallet. They are only released to the seller after the issuing company confirms the transfer of shares in their registry.
What are the platform fees?
ShareTrade charges a flat 1.5% commission on successful trades. There are no fees for maintaining an account or browsing the marketplace.
How long does a trade take to complete?
Typically, trades are completed within 24-48 hours, depending on how quickly the issuing company approves the transfer request.
Is ShareTrade regulated?
Yes, we operate in full compliance with local financial regulations and maintain strict KYC/AML verification for all users.
