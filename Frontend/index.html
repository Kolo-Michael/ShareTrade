About Us

Our Story

ShareTrade P2P was founded with one mission: to democratize access to private markets. For too long, private share trading has been opaque, slow, and restricted to a privileged few. We built a platform that changes that.

By combining escrow-backed security, company-verified trade confirmation, and a seamless investor experience, we have created the most trustworthy P2P share trading ecosystem available today.

"We believe every investor deserves direct, transparent access to private company shares — and every company deserves full control over its cap table."
